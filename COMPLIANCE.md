# Compliance and venue access

OddsFox Execution is independent software and is not affiliated with,
sponsored by, or endorsed by Polymarket.

The executor checks geographic availability from its own egress address at
startup and during live operation. `BLOCKED`, close-only, malformed, stale, or
unavailable eligibility results prohibit new orders. The software does not
provide proxy, VPN, alternate-egress, or other bypass routing.

An MIT license permits use, modification, and distribution of this source
code. It does not:

- grant a Polymarket account or API access;
- override Polymarket terms, rules, or technical controls;
- determine whether prediction-market trading is legal for an operator;
- satisfy licensing, registration, reporting, sanctions, tax, or consumer
  protection duties; or
- transfer responsibility for wallet custody or trading losses.

Operators must review current venue terms and applicable law for every user,
entity, jurisdiction, and deployment. Obtain qualified legal advice where
needed. If eligibility or authority is uncertain, do not enable live mode.
